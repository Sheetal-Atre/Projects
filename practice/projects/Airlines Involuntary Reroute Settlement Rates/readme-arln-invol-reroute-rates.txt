Receiving carriers can bill the issuing carriers for involuntary reroute tickets for lowest applicable published fare. 
This results in disputes between airlines, and about 5 million rejections annually. 
Rejections may take a long time(more than 1 year) to settle, which is a direct loss to the airline. 
The module samples and calculate the region-wise rates for the participating airlines, 
which they can use to settle involuntary reroutes in a neutral, simple, fast and dispute free method.
It also predicts the expected future rate with high confidence level.

Get 1% sample of each participant
find each cpn's standard and revenue share and weighted mileage factor
divide cpn into one of 8 global regions based on O&D
for each cpn based on clearance month and region, apply the settlement rate to weighted mileage factor
